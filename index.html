+

Is the service available 24/7?
+

Yes! whatAride operates 24/7 including all public holidays. We specialize in early-morning and late-night airport trips.

Are there hidden charges or toll fees?
+

None at all. Your quoted fare includes all tolls, state permit fees and driver bata. You pay exactly what's quoted — no surprises.

What vehicles are available?
+

Hatchbacks (Maruti Swift, Tata Altroz), Sedans (Honda City, Swift Dzire), Toyota Innova Crysta for groups. All AC and well-maintained.

What is the Bangalore to Mysore cab fare?
+

~145 km. Contact us for the latest rates — we offer the best prices with no hidden charges. WhatsApp or call for an instant quote.

Do you offer intercity cabs from Bangalore?
+

Yes! One-way and round-trip cabs to Mysore, Coorg, Ooty, Chennai, Chikmagalur, Hampi, Goa and many more South India destinations.

How early should I book an airport cab?
+

We recommend 1–2 hours before pickup. For early-morning flights, book the night before. Last-minute bookings accepted based on availability.

+

Yes — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, net banking and cash. Pay in advance or at trip end.
